COD MW III

Action

General Information

Developer: Activision

Release: 2023

Platforms:

Description

Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 (COD MW2) is a first-person shooter game developed by Infinity Ward and published by Activision. It is a sequel to Call of Duty 4: Modern Warfare. Released in 2009, it features a gripping single-player campaign and a popular multiplayer mode. The game is set in a near-future fictional world, with intense combat, modern weapons, and a storyline revolving around conflicts between the United States and Russia. Modern Warfare 2 received critical acclaim and is known for its action-packed gameplay and addictive multiplayer experience.
