Game Day X

Who Are We

Game Day X - A Celebration of Gaming.

Game Day X is an exciting gaming event that is set to take place in the beautiful city of Hadramout Al-Mukalla, Yemen. This event aims to bring together gaming enthusiasts, professionals, and casual gamers from all across the region for a day filled with thrilling competitions, interactive exhibits, and a celebration of the gaming culture.

Date: 24 April

Time: 6:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Location: AL-Dis / Al-Mukalla / Hadramout
